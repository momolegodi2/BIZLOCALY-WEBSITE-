Our Story

Connecting communities with local businesses since 2024

Our Mission

To empower local retail businesses by providing a comprehensive digital directory that connects them with nearby customers, fostering community growth and economic sustainability.

Our Vision

A world where local businesses thrive through digital accessibility, creating vibrant, self-sustaining communities across every neighborhood.

Our History

BizLocaly was founded in 2024 by a team of small business advocates who recognized the challenges local retailers face in the digital age. Starting with just 50 stores in one city, we've grown to feature over 5,000 local businesses across 25 cities nationwide.
